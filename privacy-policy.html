1. Introduction

Welcome to Traveia ("we," "our," or "us"). Traveia is a personalized travel recommendation application that helps users discover destinations matching their mood, interests, budget, and travel style.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and web platform (collectively, the "Service"). Please read this policy carefully. If you disagree with its terms, please discontinue use of the Service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: Email address, display name, gender, country of residence, and education level
  • Profile Data: Travel preferences, mood selections, interest tags, and budget preferences entered during onboarding or quiz flows
  • User-Generated Content: Any travel notes, saved destinations, or feedback you submit

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: Pages or screens viewed, features used, time spent in the app, quiz responses, and destination interactions (views, saves, shares)
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information
  • Log Data: IP address, browser type, access timestamps, and crash reports
  • Location Data: We do not collect precise GPS location. We may infer approximate region based on your stated country of residence or IP address solely to improve recommendation relevance

2.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Authentication Providers (Supabase): If you register via email authentication, your credentials are processed and stored securely through Supabase's infrastructure
  • AI Service Providers: When you request a destination "Micro Guide," your query (destination name and preference context) is sent to Google's Gemini API to generate personalized content. No personally identifiable information beyond travel context is transmitted

4.2 Academic and Scientific Research

Traveia is developed in an academic research context focused on personalized tourism recommendation systems, semantic matching, and user behavior analysis.

How this works in practice:
  • While you use the Service, your preferences and interactions are stored in association with your account (via an internal identifier) so that we can operate the Service and personalize your experience.
  • Before any data is used for research, it is de-identified: direct identifiers such as your email address and display name are removed, and data is aggregated across many users.
  • Research datasets and any published outputs contain only aggregated behavioral patterns (e.g., "X% of users with an Adventurous mood preference also selected Nature and Photography tags") — never individual-level records, and never data traceable back to a specific person.
  • We only include your data in research datasets if you have provided explicit consent during registration (see Section 6 for how to withdraw this consent at any time).

Your rights regarding research use: You may withdraw consent for your data being included in future research datasets at any time by contacting traveiapp.official@gmail.com, or via your in-app settings. Already-published aggregated results, which by definition cannot be traced back to you, cannot be retroactively altered.

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or governmental authority, or when we believe disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.

4.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you via email or in-app notice before your information becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

5. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained until account deletion, plus 30 days for backup purposes
  • Usage/analytics data: Retained in identifiable form while your account is active; de-identified and aggregated for research or long-term analytics after 24 months
  • AI query logs: Not retained beyond the immediate API call (no storage of Gemini inputs/outputs on our side)

7. Account Deletion

You may request deletion of your Traveia account at any time by emailing traveiapp.official@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request." We will process your request within 7 business days and confirm deletion via email.

  • Your account and profile data will be permanently removed
  • De-identified, aggregated usage data already included in research datasets (only if you had given consent) may be retained
  • Backup copies will be purged within 30 days

8. Data Security

  • Encryption in transit: All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS
  • Encryption at rest: Database contents are encrypted via Supabase's managed infrastructure
  • Access controls: Row-Level Security (RLS) policies ensure users can only access their own data
  • API key security: Third-party API keys are managed server-side via Supabase Edge Functions and are never exposed to the client

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security and encourage you to use strong, unique passwords.
